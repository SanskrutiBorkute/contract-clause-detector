## Abstract

This project presents **ClauseGuard AI**, an AI-powered Contract Clause Anomaly Detector designed to analyze legal contracts and identify potentially risky, unfair, or missing clauses. Traditional contract review requires significant manual effort and legal expertise, making it time-consuming and expensive.

The system utilizes Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Machine Learning techniques to automatically extract clauses, classify them, assess contract risk, detect missing legal protections, and generate plain-language explanations along with suggested clause improvements. It also produces downloadable analysis reports to assist users in understanding complex legal documents.

The results demonstrate that the system significantly reduces contract review time while improving the accuracy and accessibility of legal document analysis.

---

## 1. Introduction

Contracts form the foundation of business and legal agreements, but understanding legal language can be difficult for ordinary users. Many people unknowingly sign contracts containing unfair clauses or missing legal protections.

ClauseGuard AI aims to simplify contract analysis by automatically extracting contract clauses, evaluating their risk level, identifying anomalies, detecting missing protections, and generating AI-powered recommendations. The objective is to make contract review faster, more reliable, and easier for both individuals and organizations.

---

## 3. Methodology

The system accepts contracts in PDF, DOCX, TXT, and image formats. The uploaded document is processed using text extraction and OCR techniques when required. NLP algorithms segment the contract into individual clauses, classify each clause into legal categories, and evaluate their risk using AI-based detection rules.

The application identifies risky clauses, detects missing legal protections, generates suggested clause rewrites, calculates an overall contract risk score, and produces a detailed downloadable PDF report containing the complete analysis.

## 6. Limitations

* Complex legal interpretations may still require professional legal review.
* OCR accuracy may decrease for low-quality scanned documents.
* The system currently focuses primarily on English-language contracts.
