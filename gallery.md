# Project Gallery

This gallery showcases the major pages and functionalities of the **AI-Based Contract Clause Anomaly Detector (ClauseGuard AI)**.

---

# 1. Dashboard

This is the main landing page of the application and the first screen users see after opening the website. It provides an intuitive interface where users can upload contracts in PDF, DOCX, or image format. The dashboard introduces the AI-powered legal assistant and allows users to begin contract analysis with a single click.

![Dashboard](screenshots/Dashboard%20Page.png)

---

# 2. Contract Analysis

After a contract is uploaded, the system extracts all clauses and displays them alongside the original document. Each clause is automatically analyzed and classified as **Safe**, **Warning**, **High Risk**, or **Critical**, while also calculating an overall contract risk score.

![Contract Analysis](screenshots/Analyze%20Contract%20page.png)

---

# 3. Clause Details

Selecting any clause opens the detailed analysis page. This screen explains the selected clause in simple language and provides AI-generated insights including confidence score, suggested rewrite, legal references, negotiation advice, and legal impact.

![Clause Details](screenshots/Clause%20Details%20Page.png)

---

# 4. AI Recommendations & Missing Protections

This page identifies important legal protections that are missing from the contract. It also generates AI-powered recommendations suggesting clauses that should be added or modified to strengthen the agreement and reduce legal risks.

![AI Recommendations](screenshots/AI%20Recommendation.png)

---

# 5. Risk Analytics

The Risk Analytics page summarizes all detected risks within the contract. It provides a visual overview of flagged clauses and displays the distribution of safe, warning, high-risk, and critical clauses to help users quickly understand the contract's overall risk profile.

![Risk Analytics](screenshots/Risk%20Analytics.png)

---


# 6. Summary Report

The Summary Report presents the final AI-generated assessment of the contract. It includes the executive summary, overall risk score, final recommendation, AI confidence score, processing time, total clauses analyzed, missing protections, and downloadable PDF report for future reference.

![Summary Report](screenshots/Summary%20Report.png)
